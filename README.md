# 📊 E-Commerce Business Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an E-Commerce Superstore dataset using Python and Power BI to uncover meaningful business insights. It focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, regional analysis, profitability, and business decision-making through data visualization and analytics.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance
- Calculate Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Identify top-performing regions, states, cities, and products
- Analyze customer segments
- Track monthly sales trends
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profit
- Generate business insights and recommendations

## 📊 Business Insights

- West Region generated the highest overall sales.
- Technology category recorded the highest revenue.
- Consumer segment contributed the highest sales.
- New York City generated the highest revenue.
- Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode.
- Higher discounts negatively affected profitability.
- A small number of customers contributed a significant portion of total sales.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing products.
- Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing regions.
- Reduce excessive discounts to improve profitability.
- Strengthen customer loyalty programs.
- Improve logistics for faster shipping services.
